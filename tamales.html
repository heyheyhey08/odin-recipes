Tamales

Tamales are a unique Pre-Columbian dish that is believed to have originated in Mesoamerica, the land between North and South America. Mexican tamales are perhaps the best-known version, however, almost all of the Central and South American cultures have adopted the dish into their own style of cooking

List of ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Masa Harina
  • Pork Lard
  • Corn Husks
  • Mexican Cheese

Steps:

  1. Start by preparing the dough.
  2. Next, prepare the tamale fillings that you are going to use.
  3. Then, prepare the corn husks and wrap the tamales, which can be a fun activity for your family and guests.
  4. Steam them in a large steamer for 90 minutes.
